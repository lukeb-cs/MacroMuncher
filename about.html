About MacroMuncher

MacroMuncher is a website built to make tracking calories and improving overall health easy. Without advertisements, subscriptions, or data collection, count your calories and accomplish your fitness goals in just a couple minutes each day. Data from the app is stored locally in your browser which means that no one will be using this site to track your personal preferences or other information. This project is entirely open-source and can be found on my public GitHub account. Freely explore an easy health solution with MacroMuncher! Understand, however, that this site is not a substitute for real medical advice from real doctors; it's a passion project made by a health-focused computer science student.

About Me

My name is Luke Brenningmeyer, and I'm the creator of MacroMuncher. At the moment, I'm pursuing a Computer Science Bachelor's Degree from James Madison University alongside both a robotics and honors interdisciplinary studies minor. This project, made with the CSS, JavaScript, and HTML languages, serves to showcase my understanding of online web development. This website also satisfies a couple personal interests of mine: nutrition and health. Using many calorie tracking websites and applications in the past myself, I'm not satisfied with the average individual's accessability to health resources. Tracking your caloric or macro-nutrient intake should be easy for the average person.
